## Capture Notes

- Kled is currently the clearest primary-source example of a 2026 data marketplace that pairs opt-in supply, direct rewards, automated quality enforcement, and global payout expansion.
- Taskrabbit, Uber, Lyft, and DoorDash all show city-specific gating or reward variation rather than one national incentive shape.
- The strongest trust and quality signals come from product gates, first-task or first-trip thresholds, city-based eligibility, and restricted communication paths.
- These sources are enough to build a Blueprint-relevant set of first questions without overfitting to any one company narrative.
