# Durham Robot Team Target Accounts and Buyer Clusters

## Summary

Durham is a narrow proof-led robot-team market. The strongest target accounts are tied to a subscale logistics-bay proof motion, an AMR pre-deployment evaluation workflow, and a buyer lane that stays research-only until recipient-backed contacts are verified.

Blueprint should not lead with generic robotics outreach or broad city awareness. It should lead with one bounded site, one workflow, and one proof path:

1. site lane: class-A logistics and industrial interiors
2. workflow lane: AMR pre-deployment evaluation
3. buyer proof path: exact_site
