## Prompt Notes

The final prompts asked for:

- a compact standalone capital `B` mark built from hard geometric evidence-block shapes
- a restrained horizontal Blueprint wordmark lockup
- exact-site evidence and hosted review room imagery with `SAMPLE`, `PROOF`, `CAPTURE`, `HOSTED REVIEW`, `RIGHTS`, and `FRESHNESS` labels only
- no customer names, partner logos, performance scores, fake dates, fake hashes, fake readiness claims, generic SaaS dashboards, purple-blue AI gradients, soft orbs, robot mascots, or literal blueprint/map motifs

## Maintenance Rule

Use the SVG mark for production UI and favicon legibility. Use the generated raster boards and candidates as direction evidence, OG imagery, and future visual reference. Any future public imagery should preserve sample/proof labels and avoid implying customer proof or hosted readiness that the repo cannot verify.
