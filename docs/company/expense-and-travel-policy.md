# Expense And Travel Policy

Status: Draft, requires counsel/finance/PEO review before reimbursement use.
Owner: Finance Support Agent with Founder/CEO.
Scope: Business expenses, travel, field capture trips, buyer/vendor meetings, conferences, and remote-work expenses.
Review cadence: Quarterly, and before any recurring spend program.

## Purpose

Blueprint should spend deliberately, document evidence, and avoid accidental commitments. Expenses must support capture supply, site-specific world-model packages, hosted access, buyer workflows, reliability, or company operations.

## Approval Principles

- No one may commit Blueprint to spend outside their written authority.
- Expenses must have a business purpose, receipt, owner, and approval path.
- Travel should be tied to a capture, buyer, site, vendor, hiring, or fundraising objective.
- Vendor subscriptions follow procurement review, not informal expense approval.
- Legal, finance, payroll, benefits, tax, and insurance spend should be approved by founder/finance owner.

## Pre-Approval

Pre-approval is required for:

- travel;
- lodging;
- airfare or rail;
- conferences and events;
- equipment;
- vendor subscriptions;
- ads or outbound spend;
- meals above the approved limit once set;
- any expense involving customer/capture/site data or security tools.

If no limit has been approved for a category, require founder or finance owner approval.

## Reimbursable Expenses

Potentially reimbursable with approval:

- travel to approved site capture, buyer, investor, vendor, or hiring meetings;
- reasonable lodging and ground transportation;
- required software/tools after procurement approval;
- approved equipment;
- approved shipping for company equipment;
- approved field-capture supplies;
- business meals tied to a documented meeting or trip.

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses

Blueprint will not reimburse:

- personal entertainment;
- fines or traffic violations;
- alcohol unless pre-approved for a specific business event;
- family or companion travel;
- upgrades not required for business;
- unapproved subscriptions;
- expenses with missing receipts unless finance approves an exception;
- spend used to create fake traction, fake recipients, or unsupported product claims.

## Travel Procedure

1. Document business purpose, destination, dates, owner, and expected outcome.
2. Confirm whether the trip touches capture, buyer, site, vendor, hiring, or fundraising work.
3. Get approval before booking.
4. Keep receipts.
5. After travel, record the outcome and evidence path in the owning Paperclip issue or Notion review surface.

## Field Capture Travel

Field capture travel must also preserve:

- lawful access posture;
- rights/privacy/consent requirements;
- capture metadata and provenance;
- equipment security;
- site-contact notes and any restrictions.

## Reimbursement Submission

A reimbursement request should include:

- employee/contractor name;
- date;
- vendor;
- amount;
- category;
- receipt;
- business purpose;
- approval reference;
- customer/site/project link when relevant.

Payroll/PEO/finance owner controls payment timing and tax treatment.

## Evidence Paths

- Expense request: finance tracker or restricted Paperclip issue.
- Travel outcome: owning Paperclip issue, Work Queue row, or buyer/site/capture artifact.
- Vendor subscription: procurement record.
- Policy: `docs/company/expense-and-travel-policy.md`.
