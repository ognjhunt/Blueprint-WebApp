Proof Boundary Adjustment

I can loosen creative freedom, but not truth claims that look real to buyers, capturers, Meta reviewers, or regulators.

Not Allowed As Real Claims

Fabricated proof

Fake earnings, customer names, captured sites, press logos, real-app screenshots, robot-team outcomes, or permission status.

Why this stays blocked

This is exactly where Medvi-style speed becomes regulatory and trust risk. Blueprint's moat depends on provenance, rights, and real-site truth.

Allowed Alternatives

Labeled dramatization

“Example route,” “concept capture,” “illustrative payout path,” “simulated UI,” fictional public-indoor scenes, fictional characters.

Evidence-backed claims

Real payout bands once approved, real app screenshots from the current build, real captures once available, real customer/site names only with rights.

Harness Rule

The generator may create persuasive fictional ad scenes. The reviewer must fail any draft that presents synthetic proof as if it already happened.
