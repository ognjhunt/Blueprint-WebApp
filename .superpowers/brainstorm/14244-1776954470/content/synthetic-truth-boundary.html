Synthetic Creative Boundary

The harness can move fast with generated visuals while still preserving Blueprint truth.

Allowed To Invent

Illustrative scenes

Public-facing indoor locations, fictional capturers, stylized phone or glasses POV, generic storefronts, gyms, cafes, retail aisles, lobbies, libraries, museums, atriums.

Ad-native hooks

Fast cuts, iPhone-style overlays, “POV: you capture a public indoor space,” creator-economy framing, visual before/after of workflow complexity only.

Must Not Invent

Proof claims

No fake earnings, fake customer names, fake captured sites, fake press logos, fake app screenshots, fake robot-team outcomes, or fake permission status.

Permission-sensitive settings

No warehouse, facility, back-of-house, or private interior implication unless a real rights/provenance record supports it.

V1 Creative Label

Generated visuals are concept ads for the capture network and hosted-review product. The campaign can sell the opportunity and workflow, but every measurable claim stays evidence-backed or omitted.
