Ad Studio Harness · Gates + Failure Modes

The harness should fail closed before Meta draft creation, and Meta drafts should never be live by default.

Gate 1

Brief Safety

Reject if lane, CTA, budget cap, allowed claims, blocked claims, or synthetic label policy is missing.

Status: blocked_missing_brief_contract

Gate 2

Asset Integrity

Reject if frames, video file, prompts, provider ids, or preview thumbnails are missing or unreadable.

Status: blocked_asset_incomplete

Gate 3

Claims Review

Reject if the draft implies real proof, earnings, permission, customers, press, app state, or outcomes without evidence.

Status: failed_claims_review

Gate 4

Meta Draft Safety

Create campaign/ad set/ad objects as paused only; verify delivery status, spend cap, and response ids after write.

Status: draft_created_paused

Live Activation

Out of scope for v1 automation. The system can present a ready draft and exact activation checklist, but spend/live toggles remain human-gated.
