Ad Studio Harness · System Flow

A two-lane creative engine that produces paused Meta campaign drafts while keeping proof claims evidence-gated.

V1 Pipeline
1 · Intake

Campaign Request

Lane, city, target audience, budget cap, CTA, product surface.

2 · Strategy

Brief Builder

Growth-owned concept, hook, allowed claims, blocked claims.

3 · Visuals

Codex Image Task

gpt-image-2 beginning frames, ending frames, characters, overlays.

4 · Video

Seedance Task

OpenRouter video pass from approved frames and motion prompt.

5 · QA

Creative Reviewer

Truth, quality, platform policy, caption, CTA, output readiness.

6 · Draft

Meta Writer

Create paused campaigns/ad sets/ads with no spend enabled.

Capturer Lane

Visual style: iPhone-style capture POV, Meta-glasses POV, creator workflow, public indoor spaces.

CTA: apply to capture public indoor spaces or join the local capture network.

Proof rule: payout claims are illustrative until backed by actual approved payout data.

Robot-Team Buyer Lane

Visual style: exact-site review workflow, simulated walkthrough, hosted-review payoff, deployment-risk cutdown.

CTA: request an exact-site hosted review or join the buyer waitlist.

Proof rule: no real buyer outcomes or site claims until backed by real artifacts.

Control Point

Every generated campaign carries a claims ledger. The Meta writer only runs after QA marks the creative as draft-safe and spend-disabled.
