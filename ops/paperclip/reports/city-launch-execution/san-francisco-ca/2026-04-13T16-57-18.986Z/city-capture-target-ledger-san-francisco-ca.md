## Workflow Priorities

- Warehouse / fulfillment / parcel / 3PL (tier_1): Bay Area robot teams still care about brownfield warehouse and parcel environments, especially when proof needs to be technically inspectable fast.
- Advanced manufacturing / electronics / robotics production (tier_1): The Bay Area keeps a denser robotics and advanced manufacturing ecosystem than Austin, which makes manufacturing-adjacent exact-site proof strategically important.
- Industrial inspection and utility environments (tier_2): Inspection remains a live workflow category for technical robot teams and gives strong exact-site review value.
- Port, cargo, and airport logistics (tier_2): Oakland and SFO-adjacent logistics environments are strong exact-site and adjacent-site proof lanes for logistics robotics buyers.
- Labs, demo facilities, and technical pilot environments (tier_3): Useful for high-trust buyer discovery, but lower priority than commercially real logistics and manufacturing sites.
