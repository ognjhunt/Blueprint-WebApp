# San Jose Standard Commercial Response Pack

- status: draft only
- purpose: response-handling packet for San Jose pricing and packaging replies; not evidence of live negotiation, approved pricing, or a commercial commitment

## Response Lane

- standard commercial handling stays with the automated commercial policy
- routine buyer questions about fit, scope, timing, and published package shape stay in the standard lane
- ad hoc discounts, free work requests, retroactive credit requests, custom scope, legal language, exclusivity, and any commitment outside the approved offer are non-standard
- manual review is reserved for non-standard commitments, not routine pricing or packaging questions

## Truth Packet Minimums

- buyer name
- company name
- request type
- package lane
- discount request status
- owner
- blocker
- next action

## Boundaries

- keep the first reply focused on fit, blocker, owner, and next action
- do not imply approval that has not been given
- do not introduce pricing, rights, privacy, or revenue-share language unless a human gate is explicit
- do not widen the thread into manual review unless the request is non-standard
