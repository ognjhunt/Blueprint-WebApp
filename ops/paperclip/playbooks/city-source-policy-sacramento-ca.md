# Sacramento, CA Source Policy

## Status
- phase: draft
- owner: growth-lead
- last-reviewed: 2026-04-14
- scope: bounded Sacramento pilot

## Purpose
Keep Sacramento sourcing narrow, truthful, and reversible.

This policy distinguishes:
- private controlled interiors, which require explicit authorization and a stricter lawful-access path
- public, non-controlled commercial locations, which may use bounded online community sourcing when the brief stays inside public-area-only rules

The policy exists to keep routine invite and access-code decisions inside Growth Lead and Ops Lead guardrails while avoiding founder escalation for ordinary channel execution.

## Policy Boundary
- Applies only to the Sacramento bounded launch posture and the Exact-Site Hosted Review wedge.
- Does not authorize broader city launch claims, spend expansion, or new public posture.
- Does not authorize blanket permission claims for any site, operator, or community.

## Allowed Channels

### Private controlled interiors
Use these channels for warehouse, facility, and other controlled-interior capture paths:
- buyer-linked exact-site requests
- site-operator introductions
- direct outreach to named buyers, operators, integrators, and tenants
- curated outreach to surveying, AEC, industrial inspection, and commercial mapping operators
- approved referrals from already approved capturers or trusted operator contacts

### Public, non-controlled commercial lane
Use these channels only for public-area-only capture of places such as groceries, retail stores, and similar walk-in sites:
- local city or neighborhood groups
- neighborhood forums and local community boards
- retail and shopping communities
- creator and mapper communities
- campus communities and lightweight gig or local worker networks
- bounded Slack, Discord, Facebook Groups, Reddit local communities, LinkedIn local or industry groups, and similar public online habitats

## Disallowed Channels
- public bounty posts for private controlled interiors
- generic gig-worker recruiting for private interiors
- broad open-city recruiting before the gated cohort is working
- earnings-led, volume-led, or compensation-led copy that outruns current proof
- blanket access claims, blanket permission claims, or claims of guaranteed site access
- any channel expansion that would blur public-area-only capture with private-interior access
- paid acquisition or referral rewards unless a human explicitly approves a new spend posture

## Referral Rules
- Only approved capturers who have passed a first capture may make referrals into the supply loop.
- Referrals must preserve the original source bucket and access posture.
- Referrals into the public commercial lane must remain public-area-only and must not imply private-interior access.
- No referral bonus, incentive, or compensation change is valid without human approval.

## Invite And Access-Code Authority
- Growth Lead may issue or revoke invites and access codes within this written policy.
- Ops Lead co-owns guardrails for routine invite/access-code posture and intake alignment.
- Founder approval is required only when the policy would expand spend, public posture, channel scope, or precedent-setting exceptions.
- Any new template, invite flow, or channel class must stay inside this document until human review changes it.

## Source-Tagging Rules
Every Sacramento reply, applicant signal, or referral must be tagged with:
- city
- lane
- source bucket
- current access posture
- next owner

The canonical next-owner handoff must be visible in the intake queue or ledger path, not just in an agent comment.

## Operating Notes
- Private controlled interiors stay on the stricter lawful-access path until explicit authorization exists.
- Public commercial sourcing is allowed only when the copy stays bounded to public areas, privacy and signage are preserved, and provenance remains truthful.
- Keep public commercial sourcing to online habitats where local public-area capture conversations already happen, rather than inventing a new broad campaign.
- Avoid widening the tree. If the active core can absorb the work, keep it there.

## Approved Sacramento Public-Commercial Habitats
Use bounded placements in:
- local Facebook Groups
- Reddit local or city subreddits
- Nextdoor and neighborhood forums
- local creator, mapping, and maker communities
- campus or alumni groups
- local LinkedIn groups for retail, creator, or community activity
- small public Slack or Discord communities where public-area-only capture fits the norms

These habitats are for discovery and routing only. They do not imply private access, rights clearance, or a promise of work volume.

## Human Gate Summary
Escalate only when one of these changes:
- spend posture
- public posture
- channel scope
- rights or privacy precedent
- non-standard commercial commitment

## Working Rule
Routine Sacramento invite, access-code, and source posture decisions stay with Growth Lead and Ops Lead inside this policy. If the request cannot be answered inside these guardrails, block it and escalate with the specific reason.
