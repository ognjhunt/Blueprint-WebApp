# Exact-Site Hosted Review First Target Brief

Date: 2026-04-26
Owner: `growth-lead`
Scope: first 12 robot-team targets for the 14-day Exact-Site Hosted Review pilot

## Operating Focus

For this pilot, the org is judged by five numbers:

- targets added
- recipient-backed touches approved or sent
- replies
- qualified buyer calls
- hosted-review starts

Everything else is support work.

## Buyer

Robot teams evaluating real deployment sites.

Good-fit buyers have one of these workflow pressures:

- warehouse AMR or autonomous vehicle deployment
- retail or public-facing robot route review
- healthcare or service robot route planning with privacy constraints
- industrial inspection route planning
- yard, dock, or material-movement automation

## Artifact Rule

Use one of two artifacts:

- `proof_ready_outreach`: a reviewable exact-site hosted review or sample review, with limits clearly labeled
- `demand_sourced_capture`: a city/site opportunity brief asking which site or workflow Blueprint should capture next

Do not pitch a hosted review as ready when the ledger row is `demand_sourced_capture`.

## First CTA

For proof-ready targets:

> I can send one exact-site hosted review for your team to inspect. If the workflow is close, we can scope a more relevant site next.

For demand-sourced targets:

> What site type or workflow would be worth capturing and hosting for your team first?

## Founder Review Rule

Founder time should only be used for:

- approving or editing recipient-backed drafts
- supplying real contacts from trusted relationships
- joining qualified calls
- deciding whether a rights, privacy, pricing, legal, or public-claim exception is acceptable

Founder time should not be used to review broad agent summaries that do not change a target, contact, send, reply, call, hosted-review start, or blocker.

## Blocked Claims

- Do not claim these companies are customers, partners, users, prospects with active conversations, or approved recipients.
- Do not claim live outreach happened until `outbound.status` is `sent` and `sendLedgerPath` points to a receipt.
- Do not claim a hosted review started until the ledger records a start and the hosted-review runtime or buyer-facing evidence exists.
- Do not claim paid scale is allowed until the ledger records organic replies, hosted-review starts, or qualified calls.
