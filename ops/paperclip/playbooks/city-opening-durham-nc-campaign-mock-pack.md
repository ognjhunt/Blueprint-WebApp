Blueprint is looking for Durham capturers who can help turn real commercial spaces into useful robot-team world models. Apply only if you can capture lawful public or approved-access locations and keep provenance clear.

Need a real Durham site modeled for a robot workflow? Send the site type, workflow lane, and review need. Blueprint will keep proof tied to actual capture provenance.
