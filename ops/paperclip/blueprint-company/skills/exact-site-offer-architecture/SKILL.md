# Exact-Site Offer Architecture

Use this skill when Blueprint is deciding how to frame a commercial offer for a robot-team buyer.

Primary users:

- `robot-team-growth-agent`
- `outbound-sales-agent`

## Goal

Turn buyer demand into a credible offer shape that matches current package reality, hosted-review readiness, and human-gated commercial authority.

## Offer Rules

- start from the buyer's job and proof need
- recommend the smallest truthful offer that solves that job
- keep exact-site package access and hosted review primary
- use optional trust/readiness outputs only when the buyer actually needs them
- make the human-gated parts explicit

## Required Output

Always return:

- `Offer shape`
- `Who it is for`
- `Included now`
- `Not included`
- `Proof path`
- `Risks and blockers`
- `Human-gated decisions`

## Do Not

- invent bonuses, guarantees, or urgency unsupported by reality
- imply site coverage or rights that do not exist
- create fake bundle tiers to sound more mature
- turn pricing or terms into an autonomous decision
