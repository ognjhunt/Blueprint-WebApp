# Exact-Site Messaging

Use this skill when Blueprint needs clearer copy for growth, outbound, investor, or community artifacts.

Primary users:

- `growth-lead`
- `robot-team-growth-agent`
- `conversion-agent`
- `outbound-sales-agent`
- `community-updates-agent`
- `investor-relations-agent`

## Goal

Make the message easy to understand and remember while preserving product truth, exact-site specificity, and explicit human gates.

## Messaging Rules

- lead with the one thing the audience should remember
- make the audience, problem, and site/workflow context concrete
- use proof and examples before abstraction
- keep the customer or reader as the hero and Blueprint as the guide
- prefer exact-site hosted review, package access, and provenance truth over broad “AI” language

## Required Output

When drafting or reviewing, return:

- `Core message`
- `Audience`
- `Why this matters now`
- `Concrete proof`
- `Caveats or human gates`

## Do Not

- write in generic “transform your business” language
- imply product maturity or traction the evidence does not support
- hide uncertainty behind polished copy
- make fundraising, roadmap, or capability claims that outrun current proof
