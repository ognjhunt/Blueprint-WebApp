# Exact-Site CRO Research

Use this skill when Blueprint is auditing or testing a buyer or capturer conversion surface.

Primary users:

- `growth-lead`
- `conversion-agent`

## Goal

Find the real reason visitors do not take the next meaningful action, then turn that reason into a bounded experiment or a concrete design/copy fix.

## Primary Metrics

Prefer:

- visitor -> signup
- signup -> request submitted
- request submitted -> qualified
- qualified -> proof-ready
- proof-ready -> purchase or active evaluation

Secondary metrics only matter if they support the primary funnel.

## Audit Questions

- is the exact-site offer legible?
- is the next step concrete and low-friction?
- is the proof path visible?
- are rights, availability, or hosted-review boundaries clear?
- are we attracting shallow curiosity instead of qualified demand?

## Required Output

Always return:

- `Funnel stage`
- `Likely blocker`
- `Evidence`
- `Recommended fix`
- `Guardrails`
- `How we would measure it`

## Do Not

- optimize for shallow click-through at the expense of qualified demand
- use fake scarcity, fake social proof, or manipulative urgency
- treat persuasion tricks as a substitute for missing proof
- run experiments without a rollback and guardrails
