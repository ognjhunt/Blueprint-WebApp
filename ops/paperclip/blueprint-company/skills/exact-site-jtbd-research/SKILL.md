# Exact-Site JTBD Research

Use this skill when Blueprint is researching why a robot team, deployment team, or adjacent technical buyer would engage with an exact-site package or hosted review path.

Primary users:

- `demand-intel-agent`
- `market-intel-agent`

## Goal

Understand the real progress the buyer wants to make in a specific situation, plus the proof they need before they will trust Blueprint with a real site workflow.

## Focus

Center the research on:

- one real site or site class
- one workflow lane
- one buyer problem that already exists
- one proof threshold that must be cleared

## Question Rules

- ask about past behavior, not hypothetical future interest
- ask what they use today, not what they say they might use someday
- ask what triggered the search, not what sounds exciting in theory
- ask what proof they would need to move from curiosity to evaluation

## Blueprint Output

Always return:

- `Buyer job`
- `Trigger moment`
- `Current workaround or alternative`
- `Why current options fail`
- `Proof threshold`
- `Hosted-review fit`
- `Commercial blockers`
- `Questions that still need evidence`

## Do Not

- frame the product as a generic AI platform
- treat qualification or readiness as the default headline product
- ask leading “would you buy this?” questions
- infer willingness to pay from compliments
- flatten robot-team buyers into generic enterprise personas
