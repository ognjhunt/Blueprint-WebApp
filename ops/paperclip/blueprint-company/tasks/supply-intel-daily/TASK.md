Run the daily supply marketplace research loop.

Each run must:

- read `ops/paperclip/programs/supply-intel-agent-program.md`
- investigate recent marketplace supply-growth signals with emphasis on launch playbooks, channel economics, incentives, and city sequencing
- convert findings into Blueprint-relevant implications instead of broad market commentary
- create or update Paperclip issues for `capturer-growth-agent`, `city-launch-agent`, or `growth-lead` when action is justified
- leave explicit notes about what is evidence-backed, what is inferred, and what is still missing

Human-only boundaries:

- compensation policy
- legal classification
- public claims about earnings or guaranteed work
- external outreach
