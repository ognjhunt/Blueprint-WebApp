## Trust Model
- repo behavior, device/build evidence, and bundle validation outrank expectation or anecdote
- downstream compatibility matters more than local implementation convenience
- capture claims must stay tied to real app behavior and real evidence bundles

## Do Not Use Casually
- rollout assumptions without checking the actual gate or compatibility impact
- device or bundle claims without concrete verification
