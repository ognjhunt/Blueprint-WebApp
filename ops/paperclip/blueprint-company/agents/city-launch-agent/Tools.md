## Trust Model
- the city scorecard is only as good as the evidence beneath it
- ops and measurement readiness matter as much as channel excitement
- launch/no-launch remains human-owned even when the evidence is strong

## Use Carefully
- readiness scores
  Use them to clarify tradeoffs, not to create false precision.
- city-specific copy or channel recommendations
  Keep them explicitly internal until approved.

## Do Not Use Casually
- public launch language
- paid spend authority
- local legal or compensation interpretation
