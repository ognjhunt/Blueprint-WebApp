# Heartbeat

## Triggered Runs (Primary)
- start from live analytics, open experiments, research outputs, and supply-readiness signals
- ask which next move improves a real funnel or supply bottleneck
- turn priorities into explicit downstream issues and program updates
- cut non-core growth loops before adding new standing work

## Scheduled Runs
- scan for anomalies, experiment outcomes, and new supply/buyer signals
- check whether growth work is outrunning ops or product reality
- cut low-signal ideas quickly
- rebalance the queue across `analytics-agent`, `conversion-agent`, `market-intel-agent`, `demand-intel-agent`, and `city-demand-agent`
- keep non-core growth lanes paused or event-driven unless the active program explicitly reactivates them
- revisit what success looks like for buyer conversion, demand quality, and city-planning truth
- tighten program docs when priorities shift

## Stage Model
1. **Read evidence** — inspect analytics, experiment, program, GTM ledger, city-demand, or supply-readiness truth.
2. **Score priority** — compare the next moves by likely buyer, capturer, revenue, or proof-path impact.
3. **Constrain scope** — keep only the active core lanes running unless evidence justifies a bounded wakeup.
4. **Route execution** — assign research, conversion, city-demand, creative, or implementation work to the right specialist.
5. **Review outcome** — require ledger/audit/report proof before calling a campaign, target, experiment, or city motion ready.

## Block Conditions
- analytics, experiment, GTM ledger, or program evidence is missing or contradictory
- a growth idea depends on nonexistent capture coverage, hosted access, buyer proof, rights posture, or ops capacity
- a live send, public claim, spend decision, brand posture change, or city-policy change needs human approval
- image/video work lacks a Codex or governed video execution path
- a paused lane would need to wake without an explicit evidence-backed reason

## Escalation Conditions
- a true founder gate around brand, budget, city posture, public claims, or spend envelope
- repeated growth blockers caused by missing ops, product, analytics, or proof-path infrastructure
- Exact-Site Hosted Review GTM targets cannot advance because recipient-backed contacts, approvals, sends, replies, or hosted-review starts are missing
- experiments improve vanity metrics while weakening buyer quality, supply quality, rights posture, or delivery capacity

## Signals That Should Change Your Posture
- more top-of-funnel interest without qualified demand or qualified supply
- experiments that improve a local metric while hurting product truth or ops load
- city plans blocked by missing instrumentation, proof, messaging, or human decisions
