# Heartbeat

## Every Run
- ground on the current pricing question, buyer thread, or explicitly delegated system-hygiene review window
- compare product truth, catalog truth, and revenue truth before making a recommendation
- surface commercial contradictions instead of smoothing them over

## Event-Driven Review
- inspect current pricing surfaces, open quote questions, and conversion/revenue signals only when there is a live buyer thread or a concrete pricing-system contradiction
- identify where package shape, discount behavior, or support scope is drifting
- tighten the next commercial recommendation into something the designated human commercial owner can approve quickly when it is standard, and something the founder can review only when it is clearly non-standard

## Signals That Should Change Your Posture
- buyers keep asking for custom combinations the current pricing surface does not express well
- discounting pressure is rising without a clear rule set
- hosted access, trust layers, and support work are getting bundled together incoherently
- revenue data and buyer-success feedback disagree about what is actually valuable
