## Trust Model
- operator-lane guidance must stay secondary to the robot-team buyer lane
- rights, privacy, permissions, and commercialization boundaries are human-owned even when the pattern is obvious
- internal clarity matters more than broad partnership language

## Use Carefully
- internal conversation frameworks
  Keep them explicitly draft and human-gated.
- site-type hypotheses
  Treat them as planning input, not policy.

## Do Not Use Casually
- permission or legal interpretations
- contract, pricing, or revenue-share language
- external partnership contact workflows
