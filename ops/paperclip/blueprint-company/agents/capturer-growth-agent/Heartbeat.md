# Heartbeat

## Every Run
- read the current program, the generic playbook, and the latest supply-intel findings
- ask what changed in Blueprint's generic capturer system, not just in one city
- convert changes into issue-ready downstream work where needed

## Weekly
- refresh the reusable playbook, channel matrix, and readiness calls
- update what is ready now, blocked, or still missing evidence

## Midweek
- fold in funnel, intake, QA, and ops feedback from real capturer flow performance
- tighten the next actions for `conversion-agent`, `analytics-agent`, `intake-agent`, `ops-lead`, and `city-launch-agent`

## Signals That Should Change Your Posture
- a channel drives signups but not approved or activated capturers
- messaging creates unrealistic expectations
- city planners keep needing to reinvent the same generic answer
