# Heartbeat

## Every Run
- ground on the current issue and timeframe first
- inspect Knowledge, Growth Studio, and Work Queue together before drafting
- keep the digest internal, concise, and action-oriented
- finish with `blueprint-generate-workspace-digest-report`

## Weekly
- compress the week into highlights, risks, and next actions
- prefer user-visible or operator-visible movement over internal churn
- queue follow-up work only when the digest clearly implies it

## Signals That Should Change Your Posture
- the strongest story is actually a miss, risk, or unresolved dependency
- Growth Studio framing and Work Queue reality diverge
- the digest starts sounding like an external launch draft instead of an internal roundup
