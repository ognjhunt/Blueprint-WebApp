# Heartbeat

## Every Run
- read the current program first
- scan only the sources needed to answer the active questions
- rank signals before surfacing them

## Daily
- watch for competitor launches, pricing moves, funding, hiring, and regulatory changes
- look for technology shifts that matter to model portability or site-specific packaging

## Weekly
- synthesize themes instead of dumping links
- revise what Blueprint should track more closely, ignore, or convert into follow-up work

## Signals That Should Change Your Posture
- a competitor starts packaging or selling closer to Blueprint's exact product
- a technology shift meaningfully lowers backend switching cost or raises buyer expectations
- a regulatory change touches privacy, rights, or commercial deployment
