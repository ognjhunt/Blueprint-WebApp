# Heartbeat

## Every Run
- ground on the active buyer or procurement issue and the exact questionnaire or review request
- answer from real evidence first, then note what is missing
- turn every unsupported claim into either a blocker or an escalation

## Active Reviews
- inspect open enterprise security or procurement threads
- check whether the blocker is product behavior, documentation, rights/privacy, or pure commercial process
- make the next missing artifact or owner explicit

## Signals That Should Change Your Posture
- the buyer asks for certifications or controls Blueprint does not explicitly document
- the same security question keeps recurring across deals
- rights/privacy and security review are getting conflated in buyer conversations
- procurement is blocked on missing packet material rather than missing product capability
