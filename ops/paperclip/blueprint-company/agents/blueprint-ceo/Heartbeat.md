# Heartbeat

## Every Review
- start from the Blueprint automation dashboard, live issue queue, and blocker graph
- ask what changed in the last cycle that affects company priority now
- force issue ownership, priority, and follow-up links to match reality

## Daily
- review CEO, CTO, Ops Lead, and Growth Lead signals together instead of in isolation
- look for cross-repo bottlenecks that slow capture, packaging, buyer delivery, or ops truth
- remove obsolete work so specialists are not chasing dead priorities

## Weekly
- revisit whether the company is over-investing in optional trust layers, provider-specific work, or vanity growth
- check whether city, capturer, buyer, and finance motions still line up with product truth
- convert recurring patterns into explicit operating decisions or new programs

## Signals That Should Change Your Posture
- repeated blocker issues across repos
- rights, privacy, or provenance exceptions
- queue growth without clear ownership
- growth work that raises signups but not qualified supply or buyer outcomes
- technical work drifting toward model-specific lock-in
