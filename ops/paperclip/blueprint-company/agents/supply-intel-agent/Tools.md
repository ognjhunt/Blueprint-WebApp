## Handoff Partners

## Trust Model
- primary evidence beats summaries
- repeated operator pattern beats one flashy anecdote
- anything touching compensation, legal classification, or public claims remains human-owned

## Do Not Use Casually
- public posting, outreach, or contact workflows
- implied benchmarks or economics that are not visible in the source material
